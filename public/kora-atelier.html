"I started KORA to prove that handmade can be luxurious — and luxury can be personal."

KORA Atelier was born from a quiet weekend, a handful of pipe cleaners, and an obsession with making something truly beautiful. What started as gifts for friends quickly grew into a studio beloved by hundreds of thoughtful givers.

Every bouquet, every hamper, every keepsake is assembled by hand — petal by petal, ribbon by ribbon — with the singular goal of making your recipient feel seen, loved, and celebrated.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I place an order? +

Simply click any "Buy Now" or "Design Your Surprise" button — it opens a WhatsApp chat with us directly. Tell us what you'd like, and we'll guide you through every step. No complicated forms, just a conversation.

Can I customize colors and design? +

Absolutely! Every KORA piece is made to order. You can choose your color palette, flower types, arrangement style, and add a personalized message card. We love bringing your exact vision to life.

How long does delivery take? +

Most orders are crafted and dispatched within 48 hours. Delivery timelines depend on your location — typically 2-5 business days pan-India. We also offer express options for urgent gifting needs.

Are the flowers real or artificial? +

Our signature florals are handcrafted from premium pipe cleaners — meaning they last forever and never wilt! They're not artificial plastic flowers; they're artisanal, hand-bent sculptures that look incredibly lifelike and luxurious.

Do you offer bulk or corporate gifting? +

Yes! We love working with companies on branded gifting, employee appreciation, and event giveaways. We offer special pricing for orders of 10+ pieces. Reach out to us on WhatsApp and we'll create a custom proposal.

What if I'm not happy with my order? +

Your happiness is our top priority. If anything is not perfect, chat with us on WhatsApp and we'll make it right — whether that's a replacement, a refund, or a redo. We stand behind every piece we make.
